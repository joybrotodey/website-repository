AI-driven forecasting models analyze consumption patterns, weather data, and real-time usage trends to predict energy demand with high precision. This enables utilities to:

  • Anticipate peak loads and prevent outages

  • Reduce dependency on expensive reserve power

By minimizing forecasting errors, utilities can significantly lower operational costs and energy waste.
